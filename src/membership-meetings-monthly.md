# Membership Meetings (monthly)

**Monthly General Membership Meetings**

Monthly Membership Meetings are usually held on a convenient (typically the second) **Saturday** each Month from **2:00-4:00p.**

These days, the meetings are held courtesy of Zoom. A link to the Zoom meeting is supplied upon request to: k6plh@yahoo.com

Pre-Covid restrictions, the meeting were held at the LG/MS Police Operations Center, **15900 Los Gatos Blvd**, near Blossom Hill Rd, the in the EOC room. Some meetings were a field exercise.

The Agenda is usually posted the week of the meeting.

The Agenda files are posted in 2 formats to meet different user platform needs: ppsx (requires the no charge Microsoft PPSX player), and pdf (no charge viewer is available for all platforms).

The monthly meetings are a good place to meet other members, ask questions, and participate in planning future activities.
