# Classes
## 2022 Offerings

**Ham Class for the FCC Amateur Radio "Technician" License**

This class consists of a full day review of the questions asked on the FCC Amateur Radio Technician Exam. Technician is the FCC entry-level license for amatuer operators.

The instructors review the 400+ questions in the FCC Technician License Exam question pool (which covers ten topics, and 35 sub-topics).  The actual FCC exam consists of 35 questions, one randomly selected from each of the 35 sub-topics. You need to get at least 26 correct answers to get your lifetime license (issued for 10 year periods at a time, renewable on-line without a new exam).

Los Gatos is no longer in Covid lockdown, so the one-day class will resume being offered quarterly as an in-person class, We try to schedule each Ham Cram for the Saturday immediately following Santa Clara County Fire CERT Class graduations. The next CERT Academy graduates on Nov 5, 2022.

**Practice Exams**

You should plan on taking at least one online practice exam before the class, Recognize that you are likely to answer fewer than 10 of the 35 questions correctly because you haven't taken the class or studied the information. The purpose of taking the practice exam is to get comfortable with taking the exam and knowing what you need to know to get a license. Remember, we are only trying to teach you enough to pass the exam. Your real knowledge will come from using your radio and maybe participating in our various events.

The **FCC Technician Class** license is perfect for Emergency Communications activity. The more advanced licenses are valuable for purposes in addition to Emergency Communications (such as reaching national and international distances).
